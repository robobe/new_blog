OpenHD is an open source digital FPV system for UAVs. It sends low-latency
video from the aircraft to the ground station, and can also carry telemetry and
control data over Wi-Fi based radio hardware.

OpenHD FPV is useful when you want a configurable digital video link instead of
a closed FPV system. A typical setup has an air unit on the drone, a ground unit
connected to a screen or computer, and software for viewing video and telemetry.

[read more](https://openhdfpv.org/)
